Resume

Currently in my second year at Ashoka University, I am a Biology major with a minor in Mathematics. During the past year, I have had the opportunity to really dive into evolutionary immunology and the physics of cellular functions, coupled with mathematical modelling. It was during this period of exploration that I developed an interest in dynamical living systems. Whether it’s host-pathogen interactions, infection dynamics, or the perturbations of cellular functions, I hope to study these complex dynamics across all scales.
